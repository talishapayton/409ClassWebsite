Your Course Webpage

Location

Create a new directory named 737 in the www directory of your gibson account to hold your work, and place in that directory a file called index.html. Your index.html will link all subsequent assignments. That means the URI to the index.html page will be http://people.rit.edu/yourUserID/737/

YOU MUST USE THIS DIRECTORY STRUCTURE EXACTLY

Note the organization and case of the letters - yes, uppercase letters are different characters than lowercase letters...

Description

This is the page from which all other work you do for this course will be linked. This is a "one stop center" for me to use to find everything you do for this course.

Your Info

The page must include a picture of yourself, your name, and your RIT e-mail address (make it hard for machines to "scrape" the address).

Projects Links

Make placeholders for links to your 2 individual projects (midterm and final) and your 2 group projects (midterm and final). The URIs pointing to your midterm and final projects should end up looking like this:

  • http://people.rit.edu/yourUserID/737/midterm/
  • http://people.rit.edu/yourUserID/737/final/

Exercises Links

You must also include a list, to which you will gradually add list items containing links to each of the exercises you will complete for this class.

Simplicity

Do your best to use the principles of design you learned in 741. I want to see the best you can do, but that does not mean putting in every type of bell and whistle you can imagine. Keep it as simple and clean as you can. Remember what Einstein said:

The supreme goal of all theory is to make the irreducible basic elements as simple and as few as possible without having to surrender the adequate representation of a single datum of experience. [from wikiquote]

To paraphrase:

"Everything should be made as simple as possible, but no simpler."
